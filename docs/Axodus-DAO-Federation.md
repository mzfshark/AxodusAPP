# AXODUS DAO FEDERATION MODEL

Version: 0.1 Draft  
Status: Architecture Document

---

# 1. OVERVIEW

The Axodus ecosystem operates as a federation of autonomous DAOs connected through a shared constitutional and economic framework.

Each DAO operates independently while remaining aligned with the Axodus Constitution and Root Governance.

---

# 2. FEDERATION STRUCTURE


Axodus Root DAO
    ↓
Partner DAOs
    ↓
Members / Clients / Agents

3. ROOT DAO
Responsibilities
Constitutional governance
Economic coordination
Treasury oversight
Product governance
Security standards
Ecosystem expansion
4. PARTNER DAO STRUCTURE

Each DAO contains:

Identity
Treasury
Governance
Products
Members
Agents
Investment strategies

5. DAO CREATION FLOW
Lifecycle
DAO creation
Constitution acceptance
Treasury initialization
Governance setup
Product permissions
Operational activation

6. DAO TYPES
Investment DAO

Focused on treasury growth and asset management.

Service DAO

Focused on services and client operations.

Trading DAO

Focused on trading strategies and capital management.

Media DAO

Focused on marketing and communications.

Mining DAO

Focused on mining/hashrate management.

Compute DAO

Focused on GPU and AI compute operations.

7. PRODUCT ACCESS MODEL

All products are accessed through DAO governance.

No direct product access exists outside the DAO structure.

8. PRODUCT GOVERNANCE FLOW
DAO Treasury
    ↓
Proposal
    ↓
Voting
    ↓
Execution
    ↓
Product Interaction
9. REVENUE MODEL

Revenue may originate from:

commissions
performance fees
staking rewards
marketplace activity
compute leasing
service operations
treasury growth
10. REWARD DISTRIBUTION

Each DAO defines:

member participation
share distribution
fee allocation
treasury rules
11. AGENT INTEGRATION

DAOs may operate autonomous agents for:

trading
governance analysis
treasury management
infrastructure operations
marketing
client support
12. COMPUTE INTEGRATION

DAOs may:

provide GPU resources;
consume compute;
operate inference infrastructure.
13. GOVERNANCE LIMITS

DAOs remain autonomous except where constitutional or ecosystem-wide limits apply.

14. SECURITY MODEL

The federation includes:

proposal validation;
treasury permissions;
execution limits;
emergency controls;
auditability.
15. SCALABILITY MODEL

The federation scales horizontally through the creation of new DAOs.

16. LONG-TERM OBJECTIVE

To establish a decentralized network of autonomous economic organizations operating under shared constitutional governance.
