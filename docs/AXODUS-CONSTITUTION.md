# AXODUS CONSTITUTION

Version: 0.1 Draft  
Status: Working Document  
Governance Model: DAO Federation  
Root Authority: Axodus Root DAO

---

# 1. PREAMBLE

The Axodus ecosystem exists to establish a governed, decentralized and autonomous economic network powered by DAOs, digital assets, autonomous agents and collective governance.

This Constitution defines the foundational principles, operational limits, rights, responsibilities and governance standards that regulate the Axodus ecosystem and all DAOs operating within it.

All DAOs connected to Axodus must explicitly accept and operate under this Constitution to gain access to the ecosystem products, contracts, treasury integrations and economic infrastructure.

---

# 2. CORE VALUES

## 2.1 Sovereignty
Every DAO possesses operational autonomy within the limits established by the Constitution.

## 2.2 Transparency
All treasury actions, proposals, allocations and governance decisions must be auditable.

## 2.3 Decentralization
Decision-making authority should progressively move toward community governance.

## 2.4 Economic Sustainability
The ecosystem must prioritize sustainable growth over speculative extraction.

## 2.5 Alignment
All DAOs must operate in alignment with the objectives of the Axodus ecosystem.

## 2.6 Technological Evolution
Innovation, AI integration and autonomous systems are fundamental pillars of the ecosystem.

---

# 3. AXODUS ROOT DAO

## Responsibilities

- Maintain the Constitution
- Govern ecosystem-wide parameters
- Manage Root Treasury
- Approve protocol integrations
- Define governance standards
- Protect ecosystem integrity
- Manage economic expansion policies

---

# 4. PARTNER DAO MODEL

## Requirements for Participation

A DAO must:

- Accept the Constitution
- Use approved governance contracts
- Respect treasury rules
- Respect ecosystem security standards
- Follow proposal execution policies
- Operate transparently

---

# 5. DAO RIGHTS

Partner DAOs may:

- Manage their own treasury
- Define internal governance
- Manage members and clients
- Allocate capital between Axodus products
- Operate services
- Generate revenue
- Deploy internal strategies
- Use ecosystem infrastructure

---

# 6. DAO RESPONSIBILITIES

Partner DAOs must:

- Respect constitutional limits
- Maintain operational transparency
- Avoid malicious activities
- Protect member funds
- Respect governance execution rules
- Maintain treasury accountability

---

# 7. TREASURY STRUCTURE

## Root Treasury
Responsible for ecosystem-wide operations and strategic capital allocation.

## DAO Treasury
Independent treasury controlled by each DAO.
