# AXODUS ECONOMIC MODEL

Version: 0.1 Draft  
Status: Economic Framework

---

# 1. OVERVIEW

The Axodus economic model is designed to coordinate autonomous DAOs, treasury operations, product ecosystems and decentralized infrastructure through governance and aligned incentives.

---

# 2. CORE ECONOMIC COMPONENTS

## Governance
Coordinates ecosystem-wide decisions.

## Treasury
Stores and allocates capital.

## Products
Generate activity and revenue.

## DAOs
Operate economic strategies.

## Compute
Provides infrastructure capacity.

## Agents
Automate operations.

---

# 3. ROOT ECONOMY

The Root DAO controls:

- ecosystem treasury
- constitutional governance
- strategic allocations
- ecosystem incentives

---

# 4. DAO ECONOMIES

Each DAO may operate:
- independent treasury;
- investment strategies;
- member rewards;
- revenue distribution.

---

# 5. PRODUCT ECONOMY

## Mining
Hashrate acquisition and management.

## Trading
Automated and manual strategies.

## DeFi / Staking
Yield generation.

## Marketplace
Distribution of products and services.

## Academy
Knowledge economy.

## MCP Servers
Infrastructure subscriptions.

## Compute Network
GPU and inference economy.
