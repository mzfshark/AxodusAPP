# Axodus Academy Roadmap

## Current Stage

Prototype-ready MVP consolidation.

The Academy nucleus is frontend-first and mock-only. It validates the constitutional education, Proof-of-Knowledge, governance eligibility, ACS readiness, and `$NEURONS` reward distribution experience before real contracts or token flows are introduced.

## Current Progress

Estimated nucleus progress: 79.8%.

This estimate is based on `.instructions/TASKS.md` Academy task percentages after the consolidation sprint.

## Completed MVP Capabilities

- Academy routes inside the AxodusAPP shell.
- Course explorer with URL-addressable filters.
- Course detail pages with PoK, treasury, reward, DAO recognition, and milestone visibility.
- Learning dashboard with governance alerts and tutor accountability.
- Learning workspace with lesson execution, PoK validation rail, certification state, and reward milestones.
- Progress Engine with trust, PoK readiness, Learn-to-Win hierarchy, and unlock visibility.
- Governance eligibility with DAO-aware identity and federation status.
- Reward dashboard separating Locked `$NEURONS` and Unlocked `$NEURONS`.
- Certification viewer with proof hashes, issuer, DAO verification, governance eligibility, and public verification paths.
- Academy Governance Review with treasury, certification, reward, ACS, and future-contract references.
- Reusable mock empty, loading, and error states.
- MVP flow coverage matrix for free course, paid course, lesson-to-quiz-to-reward, certification preview, Progress Engine, Governance Eligibility, and ACS readiness.

## Prototype Acceptance Focus

- Keep Academy visually institutional, sovereign, and governance-aware.
- Keep rewards framed as educational grants and contributor incentives.
- Keep free-course Locked `$NEURONS` and paid-course Unlocked `$NEURONS` visually unmistakable.
- Keep all future contract references read-only until governance and treasury approvals exist.
- Avoid generic LMS patterns, speculative token marketing, or casino-like reward presentation.

## Next Milestones

1. Responsive QA pass across mobile, tablet, and desktop.
2. Add route-level loading/error simulation hooks if API adapters are introduced.
3. Expand quiz and Proof-of-Knowledge step flows beyond read-only preview.
4. Add tutor onboarding and ACS review workspace previews.
5. Add Marketplace integration previews for vouchers, educational NFTs, licenses, and subscriptions.
6. Define backend/API adapter contracts for courses, progress, certificates, rewards, ACS, and governance review.
7. Prepare smart-contract integration plan for `PoKMinter`, `LockedNeuronsVault`, `RewardPolicy`, and `TreasuryEmissionBudget`.

## Not In Scope Yet

- Real token minting.
- Withdrawals.
- Wallet reward distribution.
- Transferable token execution.
- Real certification NFT issuance.
- Smart contract writes.
- Production ACS enforcement.
- Production treasury execution.
